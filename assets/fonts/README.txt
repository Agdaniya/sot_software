Drop Inter, Manrope, or any TTF/OTF font files here.
The app will auto-detect and use them at startup.

Download Inter from: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter
Download Manrope from: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Manrope

Required files (Inter example):
  Inter-Regular.ttf
  Inter-Medium.ttf
  Inter-SemiBold.ttf
  Inter-Bold.ttf
